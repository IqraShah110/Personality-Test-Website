Extraversion

Key Characteristics:

  • Sociability and engagement with the external world
  • Energy derived from social interactions
  • Assertiveness in social situations
  • Enthusiasm and excitement-seeking behavior

High Scorers Tend To Be:

  • Outgoing and energetic
  • Comfortable in groups and social situations
  • Confident and assertive
  • Enjoy being the center of attention

Low Scorers Tend To Be:

  • Reserved and introspective
  • Prefer solitary activities
  • Need time alone to recharge
  • More comfortable in smaller groups
Agreeableness

Key Characteristics:

  • Concern for others' well-being
  • Empathy and compassion
  • Cooperation and helpfulness
  • Trust in human nature

High Scorers Tend To Be:

  • Compassionate and caring
  • Cooperative and helpful
  • Considerate of others' feelings
  • Trusting and forgiving

Low Scorers Tend To Be:

  • More competitive
  • Sometimes skeptical of others' motives
  • More focused on self-interest
  • Direct in communication
Conscientiousness

Key Characteristics:

  • Organization and planning
  • Responsibility and reliability
  • Goal-oriented behavior
  • Attention to detail

High Scorers Tend To Be:

  • Organized and methodical
  • Responsible and reliable
  • Goal-oriented and persistent
  • Careful and detail-oriented

Low Scorers Tend To Be:

  • More flexible and spontaneous
  • Less concerned with schedules
  • More relaxed about goals
  • Sometimes careless or impulsive
Neuroticism

Key Characteristics:

  • Emotional sensitivity and stability
  • Response to stress
  • Anxiety and worry levels
  • Mood fluctuations

High Scorers Tend To Be:

  • More sensitive to stress
  • Experience more anxiety
  • More prone to mood changes
  • More self-conscious

Low Scorers Tend To Be:

  • More emotionally stable
  • Better at handling stress
  • More relaxed in general
  • Less easily upset
Openness

Key Characteristics:

  • Intellectual curiosity
  • Creativity and imagination
  • Appreciation for art and beauty
  • Willingness to try new experiences

High Scorers Tend To Be:

  • Curious and creative
  • Interested in new experiences
  • Appreciative of art and beauty
  • Open to different ideas

Low Scorers Tend To Be:

  • More traditional
  • Prefer routine and familiarity
  • More practical in approach
  • Less interested in abstract concepts
